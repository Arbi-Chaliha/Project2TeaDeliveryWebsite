We at Assam4T provide tea delivery service to the other states of India to enjoy the healthy, refreshing and finest tea of Assam

The state of Assam is the world's largest tea-growing region by production, lying on either side of the Brahmaputra River, and bordering Bhutan, Bangladesh, Myanmar and very close to China. Historically, Assam has been the second commercial tea production region after southern China, the only two regions in the world with native tea plants. Most of the currently operating tea estates in Assam are members of the Assam Branch of the Indian Tea Association (ABITA), which is the oldest and most prominent body of tea producers of India.
